About Me

THANK YOU FOR CONSIDERING ME AN OPTION IN YOUR DESIGN PROCESS!


Hi! My name is Jessica, and I am happy you are here! I have been a designer for a number of years now. I have always loved photography and have always gotten in the habit of bringing my camera with my everywhere I go. I find that there are always things to appreciate in this world and my camera has always helped me capture those moments and objects of beauty in my life. I enjoy drawing, painting, sculpting, beading, and anything really artistic that you can do with your hands.

I have always dreamed of having my own business in the art and design industry and am hoping to be able to help people along the way in my journey to accomplish my goals. If you would like to join e on that journey then let me know via social media, my contact form, phone, email, or any means that suites you best!
